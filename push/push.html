Push Day Has Begun

Push workouts focus mainly on the muscles that help you push things away from you, such as: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Push A

-2 min. between sets-
1. Bench Press. <7,7,6,4,3>
2. Bench Press. <AMRAP>
-60 sec. between sets-
3. Seated dumbell shoulder press. 5 sets: 8-12 reps
4. Dumbell skull crusher. 5 sets: 8-12 reps
-30 sec. between sets-
5. Low to high cable fly. 5 sets: 8-12 reps
6. V-bar push down. 5 sets: 8-12 reps
7. Incline dumbell rear fly. 5 sets: 8-12 reps
